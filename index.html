Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join a local chapter?

You can join a local chapter by clicking the "Join Chapter" link on the interactive map in the Our Reach section above, or by filling out the application form linked on that page.

Are the events only for technical students?

Absolutely not! While we host many tech events (like hackathons), Student Nexus offers workshops, networking events, and career fairs for students across all academic disciplines.

Is there a membership fee?

No, membership to Student Nexus is completely free for all verified students globally. We believe in providing equal access to opportunities.
